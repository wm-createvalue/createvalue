The Canadian Food Landscape

Two problems hold most Canadian food businesses back from the next level.

The opportunity in Canadian food is real. The barriers are specific. Most owners at the $2M–$20M stage aren't held back by ambition or effort — they're held back by the inability to access growth capital on the right terms, and by decisions that don't compound over time. We built our advisory to solve exactly those two things.

Problem One
Growth capital is available — but most owners can't access it

BDC, FCC, and private lenders are active in Canadian food. The gap isn't the money — it's positioning. Most owners arrive at the capital conversation unprepared, or asking for the wrong thing. The result: worse terms, smaller facilities, or no deal at all.

$140B+ Sector size — capital is here
$2M–$20M Where most food businesses stall
How we solve it

We work with you before the conversation starts — structuring your story, your numbers, and your ask so you enter from a position of strength, not need.

Problem Two
Most food businesses grow in bursts — but the wins don't stick

A good year, a smart hire, a successful product launch. But without a clear thread connecting those wins, you end up rebuilding from the same place each time. We help you grow in a way where each move builds on the last — so your business gets stronger with every decision, not just bigger.
